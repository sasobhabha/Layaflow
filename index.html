About Konnakol & Mridangam

Konnakol is the Carnatic art of vocal percussion, where rhythmic phrases are spoken using syllables like Tha, Dhi, Thom, and Nam. The Mridangam is the primary double-sided barrel drum of South India, translating these vocalizations into metallic ringing and deep resonant sliding tones.

Voice Mapping & AI Parsing

The Speech-to-Text engine listens to normal English transcriptions (e.g. "taco", "day", "tea", "tom") and uses an approximate phonetic alignment to restore the Indian classical syllables (tha, dhi, thom, etc.) based on acoustical proximity.

How to Play

  1. Click parts of the 3D-styled drum to play manually.
  2. Load a preset composition or type custom syllables.
  3. Click Play Studio to hear the synthesized drum and custom vocalizations in perfect rhythm.
